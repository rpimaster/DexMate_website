Privacy Policy

At DexMate, we respect your privacy. Here's how we handle your data:

  • We collect anonymized data via Google Analytics to help improve the website.
  • Google Analytics uses cookies to track visitor behavior. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Any personal information provided via the contact form (e.g., email addresses) is kept confidential and only used to respond to your queries.
  • We do not share your personal data with third parties, except where required by law.
  • You can contact us at dexmate.contact@gmail.com to request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
